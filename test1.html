Mannheim is unusual among German cities in that its streets and avenues are laid out in a grid pattern, leading to its nickname \"die Quadratestadt\" (\"city of the squares\"). The eighteenth century [[Mannheim Palace]], former home of the [[Prince-elector]] of the [[Electorate of the Palatinate|Palatinate]], now houses the [[University of Mannheim]].
